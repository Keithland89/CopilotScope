# ReadinessLens (Preview)

*Pre-adoption readiness — who is licensed, enabled, and ready, plus provisioning and reclamation.*

> **Status: Preview.** Built and usable; still stabilising as part of milestone M4.

## What it answers

- Who is **licensed**, **enabled**, and **ready** to use Copilot?
- Where are the **provisioning gaps** — licensed but not enabled, or enabled but never active?
- Which licences are **reclamation candidates** (assigned but unused)?

## Source tables

| Table | Role | Required? |
|---|---|---|
| Licensed users | Licence assignment & status | **Required for this lens** |
| Org data | Slicing by team / function | Optional (recommended) |

## Key measures

- **Licence status** — assigned / enabled / ready.
- **Readiness** — share of the population ready to adopt.
- **Reclamation candidates** — assigned but unused licences.
